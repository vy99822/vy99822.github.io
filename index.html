Mission Statement

The Asian American Coalition for Justice (AACJ) is dedicated to empowering and uniting the diverse Asian American community by upholding our traditional values, advocating for fair representation, and promoting a harmonious society. Through raising awareness, fostering education, and encouraging constructive dialogue, we remain committed to celebrating the rich cultural identities and heritage of Asian Americans. By nurturing an inclusive environment imbued with the wisdom of our ancestors, we strive to pave the way for everyone to thrive and contribute to a better future for all.

About Us

The Asian American Coalition for Justice (AACJ) is a nonprofit organization passionately committed to empowering and uniting the Asian American community, anchored in our traditional values. Founded by a diverse group of Asian Americans, we strive to be a beacon that connects and uplifts the voice of our vibrant community.

Our Vision

We envision a society where the Asian American community is fairly represented, appreciated, and celebrated for its cultural heritage while embracing the importance of our traditional values. We work towards building a harmonious society where all members feel included, empowered, and supported regardless of their background or ethnicity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower and unite the Asian American community by upholding our traditional values, advocating for fair representation, and striving for a harmonious society. With a strong focus on raising awareness, fostering education, and encouraging constructive dialogue, we remain committed to celebrating the rich cultural identities and heritage of Asian Americans.

Our Commitment

We are devoted to fostering an inclusive environment that transcends cultural boundaries and upholds the core values passed down by our ancestors. By nurturing understanding, cooperation, and respect among Asian Americans and people of all backgrounds, we strive to ensure everyone can thrive and contribute to a better future for all.

Our Initiatives

  • Educational workshops and seminars that highlight our cultural heritage and promote intercultural understanding.
  • Awareness campaigns discussing the challenges faced by the Asian American community and celebrating our accomplishments and contributions.
  • Networking events, forums, and conferences to foster connections and collaboration among Asian Americans and other communities.
  • Advocacy efforts for policy changes and representation to tackle systemic issues and promote a more inclusive society.

Join Us

Become a part of our growing community and take an active role in shaping our collective future. Whether you'd like to attend our events, volunteer, advocate, or support our initiatives, we invite you to join us in our mission to empower the Asian American community and build a better world founded on understanding, appreciation, and unity.

At the Asian American Coalition for Justice, we are legacy-builders, celebrating our past, championing our present, and creating a more inclusive future for generations to come.

  • The right to non-discrimination based on exercising any of your CCPA rights.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of the aforementioned rights, please contact us using the following contact information:
